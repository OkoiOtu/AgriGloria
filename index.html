About Us

AgriGloria Farms & Holdings

Naturally Raised: Our chickens grow in open, stress-free coops, ensuring freshness, flavour, and quality in every bit. AgriGloria Farms & Holdings is a forward-thinking agricultural enterprise committed to providing high-quality livestock and farm products to meet the growingdemand for healthy and sustainable food. Founded with passion for farming excellence and community development, we specialize in the rearing of pigs, chickens, and snails, and we also supply farms-fresh eggs.

Our operations are built on the values of integrity, quality, and sustainability. Every animal in our care is raised in a clean, well-managed environment that promotes healthy growth and minimizes stress. We prioritize the use of proper feeding techniques, modern farming practices, and ethical standards to ensure that our livestock and products meet the highest quality standards.
